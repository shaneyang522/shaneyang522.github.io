About Kristine
"Experiental Design centered 💡 Industrial Design trained 🔨 ⚙️"
Hello! I'm Kristine Yang. Taipei born, NYC based, recently graduated from Pratt Institute with a Bachelors of Industrial Design.
Throughout my journey as an industrial designer, I see how smart technology can be a powerful tool in changing the way we live and think. Working in art education, Industrial Design, and implementing technology in my design process, I want to propel my passion in inspiring the young generation; whether it was creating a menstrual first-aid kit for women’s healthcare or creating a toy to encourage young girls to get into STEM. My life experiences have shaped who I am today: a critical thinker, problem-solver, and an empathetic human being, and I’m excited to work with any creative crew!
